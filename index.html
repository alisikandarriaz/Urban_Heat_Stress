Description

HotEurope is a Spatial Data Infrastructure for urban heat stress monitoring across 11 CIVIS alliance cities in Europe. It integrates Landsat 8 derived Land Surface Temperature (LST), Urban Heat Island (UHI) indices and greenness data to support urban planners in identifying heat-vulnerable areas.

LST and UHI rasters are derived from Landsat 8 Band 10 data averaged over summer months (May–September) at 100m resolution for 2022 and 2025. Live weather data is sourced from METAR airport stations updated every 30 minutes via the university PostgreSQL database hosted at Department of Geoinformatics University of Salzburg.

Select a city

Use the dropdown to pick one of the 11 CIVIS alliance cities. The map flies there instantly.

Toggle layers

The left panel controls all map layers. Activate LST, UHI and UTFVI to see heat patterns. Add biodiversity and land use to understand the drivers.

Click the map

Click anywhere on the map to inspect feature values — LST, UHI, land use class and live METAR weather.

Open Analytics

Click the Analytics button to compare all 11 cities — 24h trend, population, and greenness index.

Export data

Use PDF, GeoJSON and CSV to export data. Every layer has a Metadata download button.
